PERSONAL INTRODUCTION :: Submit your Profile  
 


Over the years, so many of you have shared your concerns about the difficulty in finding a Jewish soul mate, or BESHERT. Let's face it - only 3% of the Chicago population are Jews.
I want to help make your search a little easier. As many of you know, I run the BESHERT Personal Introduction Service. Very often I match clients from my Introduction Service with singles who have previously submitted their personal profiles. A personal profile provides a brief overview of your attributes, hobbies and interests, and, of course, what you're looking for in a mate. All singles who are matched with my clients are introduced personally at no charge, with their permission, of course. Singles are contacted only if I have an appropriate match for them.

Please take a few minutes to complete the online profile. I highly recommend that you also include your picture. Singles who provide photos have a much better chances of being matched. Please note that all information you supply will be kept strictly confidential.
Make the effort to complete your profile. It may lead to meeting your BESHERT.
