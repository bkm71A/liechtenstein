  PERSONAL INTRODUCTION :: How it Works  
 


1. Meet with Elsa. A personal interview is the perfect way for her to find out about you and the person you are seeking. Of course, it's also a way for you to find out more about BESHERT. There is no obligation to join. The interview can last between 45 and 90 minutes.

2. After you join the BESHERT Personal Introduction Service, Elsa will
begin looking for potential dates for you. Often, she will email pictures and/or a personal statement from the person whom she suggests you meet.

3. If you agree to meet this person, Elsa provides information about you to your potential date.

4. Elsa will exchange first names only.

5. You are under no obligation to meet anyone Elsa suggests. If, during a phone conversation with a potential date, you determine that it would be an inappropriate match, this introduction will not count as a date.

6. Elsa will ask the man to make the initial contact. BESHERT recommends meeting for a cup of coffee, but you may wish to meet for dinner or a drink.

7. After you meet your date, Elsa requires detailed feedback from each of you. This feedback helps her understand you and your date better and, hopefully, improves the process. Also, Elsa needs to know if you or your date would like to see each other again. BESHERT do not share your feedback with your date.

Want to begin? Please call 847-853-0474 to make an appointment, for additional information or pricing options. Again, there is no charge for the interview.
